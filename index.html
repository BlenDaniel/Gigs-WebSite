Looking for Gigs in Ethiopia?

A platform that connects freelancers, professionals, and the job seeking labor force with high value clients and companies that look for quality work across Ethiopia.

Hire or get hired for Remote Jobs

On Gigs, your ad will be posted to a wide range of top freelancers, agencies and more.
• Start by posting a Gig. Tell us about your project and the specific skills required.
• A shortlist of likely candidates who are interested can apply.

In-person short time Gigs

Each project includes an online and an in-person workspace, shared by your team and your freelancer or agency, allowing you to:
• Send and receive files online. Deliver digital assets in a secure environment on Gigs or other online communication means.
• Meet up in person to get your work from your freelancer.

Volunteering Opportunities

Once you've signed up, you can post a Volunteering opportunity for your company, review matches, interview via chat, and engage your preferred candidate — all on Gigs.
